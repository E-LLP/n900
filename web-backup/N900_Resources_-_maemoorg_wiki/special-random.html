GSoC 2009/Projects/Barcode scanner and Shopping Assistant

[edit] Barcode scanner and Shopping Assistant - GSoC 2009 Project

  • Student: Amit Sethi
  • Mentor: Valério Valério
  • Repository: https://garage.maemo.org/projects/shop-mate/


[edit] Project summary

Develop a barcode reader and shopping Assistant that makes shopping a new experience. The application will read the bar-code of products using the camera on ITT devices and compare the price with other local stores, see product reviews and also build and share shopping lists, wish lists etc..


First step in the project is to develop cython bindings for the barcode library.
